Presentation

Hello, i compose, play, record and mix my own music. If you think that my work can fit in anyway with yours please contact me !

I love music...i alway do. I like to combine, associate, reveal things through that craft of sound, texture, rythme, notes... I dont have a typical background and root so i hope this site (that i develop and programm myself) will help you to get a glimpse of what i can (and not) do.

I now lives and works from Europe where i play, compose, produce and collaborate with a number of people. Rather than talking about my background i would like to talk about some people from different areas that inspire the way i see or feel music even thought there are not neccessarely related to my discipline, but i see all them as genuine artist (based on my definition of it, even if your discipline is not see as an artistic discipline, it doesn't mean that you can not practice, live it like an "artist" a kind of way of living); i would briefly present each of them here as hommage, here are the sources i keep coming back for many years now when i need inspiration.
